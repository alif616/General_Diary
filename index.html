Online General Diary System

A digital initiative by Bangladesh Police to serve citizens better with transparency and efficiency

You can use this website to file complaints with the police regarding lost or found property, or any other relevant matter. The concerned police station will take necessary steps based on your complaint type and inform you accordingly.

Complaint Process

  • If your complaint qualifies as a General Diary (GD), you will receive a digital copy with GD number and details of the investigating officer.
  • If your complaint qualifies as a case (cognizable offense), you will need to appear at the police station with a printed copy or complaint code.

Required Information

  • National ID Number
  • Active Mobile Number
  • Recent Photograph
  • Details of the incident

Benefits of Online GD System

  • Easy way to report complaints to police from anywhere
  • Check latest complaint status in real-time
  • Communicate with investigating officer online
  • Download and share GD copies digitally
  • Reduced processing time and paperwork

About Us

The Online General Diary system is an initiative by Bangladesh Police to make police services more transparent, accessible, and citizen-friendly. This platform allows you to submit General Diaries online, track complaint status, and communicate directly with investigating officers.

Our mission is to leverage technology to improve police services and build stronger trust between the police force and the community. This system reduces the need for physical visits to police stations and speeds up the process of filing and tracking General Diaries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is a General Diary (GD)?

Ans: A GD is a record kept at a police station for non-cognizable incidents or lost property.

Q2: Can I file a GD from anywhere?

Ans: Yes, as long as you have internet access and valid identification.

Q3: Is my personal information safe?

Ans: Absolutely, your information is secured and only used for official purposes.

Q4: How long does it take to process a GD?

Ans: Typically, GDs are processed within 24-48 hours after submission.
